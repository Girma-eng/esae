Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ESAE membership

Who can join ESAE?

ESAE membership is open to students, professionals, researchers, educators, and organizations involved in agricultural engineering and related fields in Ethiopia. This includes agricultural engineers, agricultural mechanization specialists, irrigation engineers, post-harvest technologists, and related professionals.

How do I apply for membership?

You can apply for membership online through our registration page. The process involves: 1) Selecting your membership category, 2) Providing personal and professional information, 3) Uploading required documents (CV, certificates), 4) Making payment, and 5) Receiving confirmation within 3-5 business days.

What documents do I need to provide?

Student members need proof of enrollment. Professional members need copies of educational certificates and CV. Corporate members need business registration and letter of authorization. All applicants need a recent passport photo and valid ID.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. Student members can upgrade to Professional upon graduation. The difference in membership fees will be prorated based on the remaining membership period.

How do I renew my membership?

Membership renewal notices are sent 30 days before expiration. You can renew online through your member portal, via bank transfer, or at ESAE offices. Early renewal discounts are available for renewing 60 days before expiration.

Are there discounts for group membership?

Yes, universities and organizations enrolling 10 or more members receive a 15% discount. Corporate members get up to 5 employee memberships included. Contact membership@esae.org for group enrollment.

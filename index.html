About ESAE

Our Mission and Vision

The Ethiopian Society of Agricultural Engineers (ESAE) is a professional organization dedicated to advancing the field of agricultural engineering in Ethiopia. Our mission is to advance the profession of agricultural engineering in Ethiopia through promotion of sustainable practices, technological innovation, knowledge sharing, and professional development that enhances food security, agricultural productivity, and environmental stewardship.

Founded in 2021, ESAE has grown to become the leading voice for agricultural engineers in Ethiopia, with over 100 members from academia, industry, and government sectors.

We work to enhance food security, improve agricultural productivity, and promote environmentally sustainable practices through the application of engineering principles and technologies.

Membership Benefits and Activities

The Ethiopian Society of Agricultural Engineers (ESAE) offers a wealth of opportunities for agricultural engineering professionals in Ethiopia.

As a member, you'll gain access to a vibrant network of colleagues, participate in cutting-edge research and development, and benefit from numerous professional development opportunities.

Our annual national conferences provide a platform to share your work, learn from experts, and connect with leaders in the field.

We regularly organize workshops and training sessions on the latest technologies and techniques, ensuring that our members remain at the forefront of the profession.

The ESAE also actively engages in advocacy, working to promote policies that support sustainable agricultural practices.

Membership provides access to exclusive resources, including publications, newsletters, and online forums, facilitating collaboration and knowledge sharing among our diverse membership.
